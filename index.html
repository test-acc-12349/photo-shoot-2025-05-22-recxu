Everything you need to know about our photography lightbox

Our lightbox accommodates products up to 12" x 12" x 12", making it perfect for most small to medium-sized items like jewelry, electronics, cosmetics, toys, and accessories.

The LED strips provide 6000K daylight-balanced lighting with adjustable brightness from 10% to 100%. This ensures accurate color reproduction and eliminates the need for color correction in post-processing.

No tools required! The lightbox unfolds in seconds with a simple pop-up design. Just unfold, plug in the USB cable, and you're ready to shoot. Folding it back down is just as easy for storage.

Each lightbox includes 6 professional backgrounds: white, black, red, blue, green, and yellow. All backgrounds are made from high-quality, wrinkle-resistant material that's easy to clean and maintain.
